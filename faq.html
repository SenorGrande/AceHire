Frequently Asked Questions


  • - What equipment do you have?
    • Check the equipment page and print off your own copy of the equipment list or click here

  • - How much is it to rent the equipment?
    • Check the equipment page for the price and everything we have to offer

  • - How long can I rent for?
    • There is a half day and a full day option

  • - Do you sell anything or is everything just for hire?
    • We do not sell any equipment on a usual basis

  • - Does Ace Hire do repairs?
    • Yes, costs may vary per problem. We can also service equipment for you. Servicing a Lawn Mower costs $45.00

  • - Can I get my chainsaw sharpened here?
    • Yes $12.00 for sharpening. $18.oo if we have to get it off your chainsaw. we can also make new chains for people.

  • - Can you deliver equipment to me if I'm unable to pick it up myself?
    • Yes we can if the location is within a reasonable distance from where we are. We will also pick it up from you if need be.

  • - If a rent a piece of equipment, am I able to keep it overnight?
    • Yes and we won't charge you any more for the overnight.

  • - How long is a Half day for?
    • A Half day is usually 4 hours long, although we are flexible around times

  • - If I hire on a Saturday, can I return on Monday? (since you are closed on Sundays)
    • Yes, Hire late Saturday, return on Monday - Minimum half day rate.
